Our Story

A journey of passion, light, and memories.

Our Founder

Rangu Purnachander

Founder & Creative Visionary

"Photography is not just our profession — it is our lifelong passion and purpose."

My journey began at the young age of 14, driven by nothing but deep curiosity and a desire to freeze moments that time could never erase. For 14 years, I honed my craft in professional studios, learning that photography is about emotion, trust, and the responsibility of capturing life’s most meaningful moments.

In 2002, I founded Pavani Studios with a vision to create a legacy of commitment and quality. Today, we stand proud having captured over 1500 weddings and nurtured countless talents who have gone on to build their own successful careers in the industry.

"We believe photographs are not just images; they are memories filled with life, entrusted to our hands to be preserved forever."
